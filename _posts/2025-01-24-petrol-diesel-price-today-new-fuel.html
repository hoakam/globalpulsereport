Petrol-Diesel Price Today: New Fuel Rates Released – Have Prices Increased or Decreased in Your City? Check Now


Petrol-Diesel Price in India: If you are planning to refuel your vehicle today, it is important to check the latest petrol and diesel prices in your city before heading to the fuel station.

New Delhi:
Petrol Diesel Price Today: The government-run oil companies have released the new petrol and diesel rates for January 23. Fuel prices in India are updated daily at 6 AM. Additionally, there are certain regions where there has been no change in fuel prices today.

If you are considering filling your vehicle’s fuel tank today, checking the latest rates in your city is essential. Fluctuations in petrol and diesel prices can directly impact your budget, making it beneficial to stay informed about the latest rates.

How to Check the Latest Petrol and Diesel Prices?

If you want to check the latest fuel prices in your city, there are several convenient methods available. You can visit the official websites of government-owned oil companies such as Indian Oil, Bharat Petroleum, and Hindustan Petroleum to get real-time price updates. These companies also offer mobile apps that allow users to check the latest fuel rates within seconds.

Additionally, oil companies provide an SMS service where you can send the PIN code of your city’s fuel station to receive the latest petrol and diesel prices directly on your phone. You can also visit NDTV’s news websites and apps to check daily updated fuel prices.
