Saif Ali Khan Injured in Knife Attack at Mumbai Home

Bollywood actor Saif Ali Khan, husband of actress Kareena Kapoor Khan, was injured in a shocking knife attack at his Bandra residence in Mumbai during the early hours of Thursday morning.

Intruder Breaks into Bandra Residence

According to police reports, an unidentified man unlawfully entered Saif Ali Khan’s home late Wednesday night. The intruder initially confronted and argued with the household maid, creating a tense situation

Saif Ali Khan Intervenes 

In an attempt to defuse the altercation, Saif Ali Khan stepped in. Unfortunately, the situation escalated, and the intruder attacked the actor with a knife.

Actor Stabbed Six Times

The assailant reportedly stabbed the actor six times during the attack. Two of the injuries are more severe, with one reportedly near his spine. Fortunately, the injuries are deemed non-life-threatening.

Medical Treatment Underway

Saif Ali Khan was immediately rushed to Lilavati Hospital in Mumbai for treatment. Doctors have confirmed that he is in stable condition and undergoing observation.

Mumbai Police Investigating the Incident

Authorities are actively investigating the case to identify the attacker and understand the motive. The police confirmed in a statement, “An unknown person entered the residence and injured Saif Ali Khan after an altercation with the maid.”

Concern Among Fans and Bollywood

The news has shocked fans and colleagues in the Bollywood industry, with many expressing their prayers for Saif’s recovery.
