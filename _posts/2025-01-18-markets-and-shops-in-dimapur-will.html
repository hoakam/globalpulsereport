 Markets and shops in Dimapur will remain closed until noon on Republic Day.until noon on Republic Day.

The Deputy Commissioner of Dimapur, Dr. Tinojongshi Chang, NCS, has ordered that all markets and shops in Dimapur stay closed until 12:00 noon on January 26, 2025. This order is to help with the Republic Day celebrations.
