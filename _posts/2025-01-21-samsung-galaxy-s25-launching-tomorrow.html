Samsung Galaxy S25 launching tomorrow: India prices of all models leaked



Samsung is set to unveil its Galaxy S25 series at the Galaxy Unpacked event on January 22, 2025. Ahead of the official launch, leaked information has surfaced regarding the pricing of these flagship smartphones in India.

Leaked Pricing Details:

  • Samsung Galaxy S25:

    • 12GB RAM + 256GB Storage: ₹84,999
    • 12GB RAM + 512GB Storage: ₹94,999

  • Samsung Galaxy S25+:

    • 12GB RAM + 256GB Storage: ₹1,04,999
    • 12GB RAM + 512GB Storage: ₹1,14,999

  • Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra:

    • 12GB RAM + 256GB Storage: ₹1,34,999
    • 16GB RAM + 512GB Storage: ₹1,44,999
    • 16GB RAM + 1TB Storage: ₹1,64,999

These leaked prices suggest a notable increase compared to the Galaxy S24 series. For instance, the base model of the Galaxy S25 is expected to start at ₹84,999, whereas the Galaxy S24 was launched at ₹74,999 for its 8GB+128GB variant.


Possible Reasons for the Price Hike:

The anticipated price increase could be attributed to several factors:

  • Enhanced Specifications: The Galaxy S25 series is rumored to feature the Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset, offering superior performance.

  • Increased Base Storage and RAM: The base models are expected to come with 12GB of RAM and 256GB of storage, an upgrade from the previous generation's 8GB RAM and 128GB storage.

Pre-Order and Availability:

Pre-orders for the Galaxy S25 series are anticipated to commence shortly after the official announcement, with deliveries expected to begin in early February. Consumers can look forward to various pre-order benefits, including potential cashback offers and storage upgrades.

Conclusion:

While these leaked prices provide an insight into the expected cost of the Galaxy S25 series in India, it's essential to await the official announcement for confirmed details. The Galaxy Unpacked event on January 22, 2025, will shed more light on the specifications, pricing, and availability of Samsung's latest flagship lineup.
