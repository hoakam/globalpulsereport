Delhi CM Oath Ceremony LIVE: Rekha Gupta Assumes Office at Delhi Secretariat


In a historic political development, Rekha Gupta has officially taken the oath as the new Chief Minister of Delhi. The swearing-in ceremony took place today, marking the beginning of a new leadership era in the national capital.

The grand event was held at Raj Bhavan, where Rekha Gupta was administered the oath of office and secrecy by the Lieutenant Governor of Delhi. The ceremony witnessed the presence of several dignitaries, political leaders, bureaucrats, and enthusiastic supporters who gathered to witness this landmark occasion.

Rekha Gupta, a prominent leader known for her strong grassroots connection and commitment to public service, expressed her gratitude to the people of Delhi for their unwavering support. In her speech, she promised to prioritize governance reforms, improved public services, and overall development in the capital.

Following the oath-taking ceremony, celebrations erupted across Delhi as party workers and supporters rejoiced at their leader's achievement. Many gathered outside the Delhi Secretariat, chanting slogans and distributing sweets. Political leaders from various parties extended their congratulations to the new Chief Minister. While some welcomed the new leadership, others expressed concerns and expectations regarding the governance policies that Rekha Gupta will implement.

After the official ceremony, CM Rekha Gupta reached the Delhi Secretariat, where she was welcomed by senior officials and staff members. She assumed charge in her office and held her first meeting with key administrative officials to discuss immediate priorities for the government. Addressing the media, she reaffirmed her commitment to tackling pressing issues such as pollution, infrastructure development, healthcare, and education reforms. "My government will work tirelessly for the people of Delhi. Transparency, accountability, and efficient governance will be our guiding principles," she stated.

With Rekha Gupta at the helm, Delhiites have high hopes for significant reforms in various sectors, including public transport and infrastructure, enhancing metro connectivity, road development, and sustainable urban planning. Education and healthcare will be strengthened by improving government schools and hospitals to ensure better facilities for citizens. Pollution control remains a major concern, with expectations of stricter measures to combat air pollution and improve environmental sustainability. Women’s safety and empowerment will also be a key focus, introducing policies that ensure a safer and more inclusive environment for women in the city.

Despite the celebratory mood, the new CM faces several challenges, including addressing civic issues, water scarcity, and balancing economic growth with environmental concerns. Political analysts believe that the coming months will be crucial in determining the effectiveness of her leadership and the fulfillment of her election promises.

As Rekha Gupta embarks on her journey as the Chief Minister of Delhi, the entire city watches with anticipation. Her leadership will play a pivotal role in shaping the future of Delhi’s governance, and citizens remain hopeful that her tenure will bring positive changes to the capital.
