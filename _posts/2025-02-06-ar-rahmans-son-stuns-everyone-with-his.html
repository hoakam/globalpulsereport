AR Rahman's son stuns everyone with his ₹3.2 crore watch while meeting Ed Sheeran at his concert in Chennai

                                                           AR Rahman and his son, AR Ameen, pose with Ed Sheeran

 AR Rahman’s son, AR Ameen, had a memorable encounter with UK singer Ed Sheeran during his concert in Chennai. For the occasion, Ameen wore an eye-catching watch valued at ₹3.2 crore.

At the concert, which took place last night, Ed Sheeran performed with AR Rahman as a special guest. The two musicians collaborated on a remix of Sheeran’s Shape of You and Rahman’s Urvasi. Rahman shared pictures on Instagram showing him alongside the “Photograph” singer, both sitting on a couch. Also in the photo was Rahman’s son, Ameen, who sported a luxurious timepiece from the renowned Swiss brand Richard Mille. Ameen wore the RM 65-01 Split Second Chronograph Titanium, a watch recognized by The Indian Horology's Instagram page. Let’s take a closer look at the price and details of Ameen’s watch.

How much does AR Rahman’s son’s watch cost?

Ameen styled the high-end watch with a black velvet shacket from Dior, featuring the brand’s logo print, a collared neckline, folded cuffs, full-length sleeves, and a relaxed fit with front button closures. He paired it with simple black pants.

The RM 65-01 Split Second Chronograph Titanium watch is priced at CHF 235,000, which is approximately ₹2.26 crore. However, according to the chrono24 website, the watch’s value is listed as ₹3.27 crore. Known for its complexity, the watch took over five years to develop. Inspired by motorsports, it offers a power reserve of over 60 hours. The watch’s baseplate and bridges are made from grade 5 titanium and feature a sapphire-blasted surface.

Celebrities who have worn Richard Mille watches

Richard Mille watches are considered symbols of luxury and have been worn by various high-profile individuals. Celebrities like Shah Rukh Khan, Anant Ambani, Ram Charan, Jr NTR, Nayanthara, and others have been seen wearing these prestigious timepieces. Ed Sheeran himself is a proud owner of a Richard Mille watch, which he wore to the 2015 Grammys.

Anant Ambani, too, has an exceptionally rare Richard Mille watch — the RM 52-04 Skull Blue Sapphire, valued at ₹22 crore. This particular watch is one of only three ever made.
