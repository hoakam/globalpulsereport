Abel Demands Another Center Forward at Palmeiras, Urges Club to Strengthen Squad

Palmeiras head coach Abel Ferreira has made it clear that he wants reinforcements in the attack, specifically another center forward, as the team prepares for the challenges ahead. The Portuguese manager emphasized the need for the club’s management to make an "effort" in securing new signings.

Abel’s Concern Over Squad Depth

Speaking to the media, Abel expressed his concerns about the squad’s depth, particularly in the striker position. Despite Palmeiras having a strong squad, he believes an additional center forward is crucial for maintaining competitiveness in multiple tournaments.

"We need reinforcements, especially in the attack. The season is long, and injuries, suspensions, and fatigue will come. If we want to keep fighting for titles, we must strengthen the team," Abel stated.

Palmeiras’ Transfer Strategy

Palmeiras has been active in the transfer market, but securing a quality striker remains a challenge. The club is reportedly evaluating several options, with discussions ongoing to bring in a prolific goal scorer. Abel has urged the board to accelerate negotiations to ensure the squad is ready for the upcoming fixtures.

"I understand the financial challenges, but if we want to keep winning, we must make an effort. The fans deserve a strong team, and we need solutions in the attack," he added.

Upcoming Challenges for Palmeiras

With Palmeiras competing in multiple tournaments, including the Brasileirão, Copa Libertadores, and domestic cups, squad rotation will be essential. Abel's demand highlights his determination to keep Palmeiras at the top, ensuring they remain title contenders on all fronts.

The coming weeks will be crucial as Palmeiras looks to secure signings and strengthen their squad ahead of a demanding season.
